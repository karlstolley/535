More Practice

Ok, so how do you know if you really understand HTML5? Try moving some of the elements around to rearrange the infrastructure of the webpage. As we've learned, some of the elements cannot and should not be moved around (e.g. head, body, main navigation, etc.); but others elements are meant to be flexible so you can build a page to suit your needs. If you are unsure about why some elements can move and others cannot, review the sections on structure.

  • What can elements could you move around to present your content in the best way?
  • Would the image look better at the top of the page?
    • How the table? Where should the table be place on the page
  • Should the unordered list be an ordered list?
